How to Order

  1. Browse the items and click "Add to Cart" to select your favorite pastries.
  2. Click on "My Order(s)" to review your cart.
  3. Fill in your contact and delivery details.
  4. Complete your order by making a secure payment.
  5. Receive a confirmation email and get ready to enjoy your treats!
