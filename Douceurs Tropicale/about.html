    About us

    Baking with love and laughter

    Head Baker

    Name: Aïcha Ndiaye

    Age: 42

    “Born in Senegal, Chef Aïcha is the visionary behind Douceur Tropicale. With over two decades of experience in luxury patisseries in Dakar and Paris, she brings refined technique and soulful flavor to every creation. Her specialty lies in reimagining traditional West African ingredients like hibiscus, ginger, and baobab into delicate pastries such as Hibiscus Mille-Feuille and Ginger-Coconut Flan.”

    Baker

    Name: Kenji Yamazaki

    Age: 35

    “Born in Kyoto, Kenji blends Japanese precision with artistic flair to create minimalist yet deeply flavorful desserts. He is the creator of the famous Coconut-Lychee Mochi Ice Cream and Matcha Cheesecake with Passion Fruit.”

    Baker

    Name: Sofia Morales

    Age: 29

    “Hailing from Colombia, Sofia brings vibrant flair and color to the bakery’s creations. An expert in Tres Leches Cake, Brazilian Brigadeiros, and Spiced Cajeta Churros, she reinvents Latin classics with bold flavors.”
